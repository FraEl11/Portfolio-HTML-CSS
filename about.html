About

Hi there everyone, I'm Francesca, a graphic and web designer with a somewhat unconventional training path.

My passion for drawing stems from my childhood as I was constantly drawing characters from my favorite cartoons and comics, especially animes and mangas. This love for Japanese animation stuck with me for a long time, so much that I ended up graduating in oriental languages, but, after university I decided to follow my more artful inclinations and started various online and real-life courses on graphic design and illustration, accompanied with ones on web design and front-end.

As I started to take my first real steps in the digital world, by making websites with CMS Wordpress for private users and institutions (like the Lazio Region), I soon found myself in need of a deeper understanding of website development, to further advance my skill and also because I'm an insatiably curious person at heart, so I took part in an online course on Udemy at first and then I started attending the Start2Impact Master on front-end development.

Meanwhile I kept illustrating and I gradually discovered the lettering discipline, that immediately struck me mesmerized and eager to study it in depth.

Here you can find all of my works and experiments, if you want to take a look.
